INTRODUCTION

The purpose of the activity was to show how energy moves from one simple machine to another

The challenge was to create a rube goldburg machine using a 2’ by 2’ piece of plywood as a base.

Constraints:

  • 24” x 24” base
  • Energy has to leave at a minimum of 4 inches
  • Needs to have all six simple machines (pulley, wheel and axle, wedge, inclined plane, screw, and lever)
  • Machine must be stored on shelfs
  • Order of machines must be as assigned

BACKGROUND

We referred to past student projects before we designed our machine. We also viewed online pictures and videos to give ideas of what kind of objects we could use in our project

MATERIALS

  • Plastic cups
  • Hot glue
  • String
  • Plywood
  • Cardboard
  • Pulley
  • Straws
